Paste a 5-color swatch from rgbcolorvis (e.g., 8A7B96,7B968A,968A7B,627C70,ADA397) or click Randomize. Click a section (sidebar, header, content, footer, or accent) to select it. Hover over a swatch to preview the color change. Click a swatch to apply the color. Drag the nav bar to reposition it (below header, left, right, or drop outside to omit). Select alignment (center or percentage). Use the export buttons to download your design as HTML+CSS, HTML only, or CSS only.
