PageVis

Paste a 5-color swatch from rgbcolorvis (e.g., 8A7B96,7B968A,968A7B,627C70,ADA397) or click Randomize. Select a section (background, header, nav, content, footer, or accent) using the dropdown or by clicking it. Hover over a swatch to preview the color change. Click a swatch to apply the color. Edit content directly in the content section (type or paste HTML). Select alignment (center or percentage). Use the export buttons to download your design as HTML+CSS, HTML only, or CSS only.

Welcome to PageVis!

1. Enter 5 hex codes or click Randomize to set colors.

2. Choose a section (background, header, nav, content, footer, accent) using the dropdown or by clicking it.

3. Hover over a swatch to preview, click to apply.

4. Edit content directly in the content section (type or paste HTML).

5. Export your design as HTML+CSS, HTML, or CSS.
